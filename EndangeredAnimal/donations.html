Donations

There are many places you can donate to so that you can help save the Amur tiger from becomming extinct.

The Alta Conservation Foundation

The Alta Conservation Foundation is a wildlife protection foundation that has fundraisers to support the Amur tiger. The fundraisers raise money to pay for the supplies and winter coats for the anti-poaching crew they host. They use non-lethal methods such as tranquilizer darts to detain poachers and protect the tiger.

Donate

WCSRussia

The WCSRussia organization, the longest running radio-telemetry based tiger project, specializes in tiger-human conflicts and training scientists for specializing in Amur tiger habits. They research and conserve the Amur tiger's’ population. They also assist the government of Russia in preserving wildlife.

Donate

World Wildlife Fund

The World Wildlife Fund supports the Amur tiger in the Amur-Heliong landscape by monitoring their population. They also prevent habitat loss and illegal poaching. They push for harsher anti-poaching and anti-deforestation laws in congress, and increase the prey the tigers have access to. They also provide a lot of information about the tiger itself, why it is poached, and why deforestation harms them. They spend a lot of time in their site raising the public’s awareness of the Amur tiger’s threats. They want the public to get involved physically as well as monitarily.
